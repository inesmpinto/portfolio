About me

I tell stories where journalism meets code and design. I love exploring how data, visuals and interactivity can reveal what words alone can’t. My work often begins with messy datasets — cleaning, analysing and shaping them into narratives that feel human, visual and intuitive. I’m especially drawn to stories about climate and how people’s lives intersect with the changing world around them.

Tools: JS, HTML5, CSS3, Adobe, Flourish, Datawrapper, Mapshaper, Mapbox
